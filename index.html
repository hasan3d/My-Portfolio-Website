How it all started?

I call myself a self-taught web developer. I did not take any CS class in university and I did not go to any university either. Whatever, I have learned so far is all from my quest to learn what I am passionate about most. Although, There was lots of ups and down in my life and sometimes for the sake of living I had to choose different occupations other than coding but I never gave up my dream to become what I want in my life. I am still fighting to pursue my dream. I started playing with computers and the web at the age of 16. First, It was writing some basic HTML for a mobile site on my Nokia phone which turned out to be a site where you could let the user download some cool contents and I very much liked that. Since then, I have become curious about that and started reading various online resources such as blogs, tutorial sites and e-books as well as paper books and video tutorials. I had spent countless hours on teaching myself coding. I still continuously do so because programming is something you have to keep practicing and repeating otherwise you tend to forget. Having said that there are lots of changes coming up each year plus new versions and so on.
